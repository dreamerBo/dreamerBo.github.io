Delivery & Shipping

We endeavour to despatch all orders within 3 busines days of verifying your payment. We generally use Australia Post eParcel for shipping, but may use Australia Post standard mail service for certain items.

Please refer to the product listing for details.We ship to address provided in eBay. Please make sure your shipping details in your eBay account are correct as we cannot guarantee that it can be changed. This is because we use an automated order processing system, so all orders will go through to our warehouse for packing once payment has been verified.


      *Delivery delays can occur for a variety of reasons. Please allow an additional 3 business days after the expected delivery time before contacting us to follow up with our delivery company. We recommend purchasing postal insurance for your parcel, this will cover you for any loss or damage in transit. Please select "with tracking and signature" postal service during checkout, for your parcel to be covered by postal insurance.

      *We do not take responsiblity for damaged or lost parcels if no insurance cover was purchased for that item.

      *We do not offer pick up as an option. We do not have a storefront, showroom or units on display.

      *We ship within Australia only.

      *No express service is available.

Return & Refund

  • Refund or replacement request is available only the requests within 1 week after parcel received & returning the item as the same condition as received.
  • Please contact us to request a return authorization. Your name, auction number, and reason for return should be included in the email.  All items returned must contain all original packaging materials.
  • Please repack the item carefully. Items returned will be tested and a new replacement will be shipped to the buyer immediately when found defective.
  • In the event that a suitable replacement is not available then a refund will be issued. Shipping, handling and insurance fees are non refundable.
  • If the item is found non-defective, the item will be sent back to the buyer at buyer's expense.  Items returned without RMA number will be refused and returned to the sender.
  • Buyer is responsible for All Return Postage and Shipping Charges.
  • Buyer is responsible for additional shipping costs for items that are returned refused and/or undeliverable.  We do not accept any returns without an authorization number on the box or label it is being returned in.
